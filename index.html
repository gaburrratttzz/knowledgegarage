Car Washing Tips

Use Two Buckets

One bucket for soap water and another for rinsing your sponge. This minimizes dirt contamination.

Wash in Sections

Divide the car into sections, like the roof, sides, and wheels. This ensures no dirt is left behind.

Dry Immediately

Use a microfiber towel to dry your car right after washing to avoid water spots.
